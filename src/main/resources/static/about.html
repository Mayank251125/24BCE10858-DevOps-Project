About the Symposium

Bringing together India's finest minds to celebrate innovation, software engineering, and scientific discovery.

Empowering the Tech Leaders of Tomorrow

Established in 2015, VIT TechFest is the annual technical symposium organized by the Vellore Institute of Technology. It serves as a national platform for engineering students to exhibit their technical skills, collaborate on state-of-the-art software systems, and interact with prominent engineers from Google, Microsoft, Netflix, and various startup hubs.

Our mission is to foster continuous innovation, build strong peer communities, and introduce students to industrial DevOps practices, platform engineering workflows, and system reliability concepts.

  • Over 35 technical, non-technical, and gaming events.
  • National-level 36-hour hackathons with cash prizes.
  • Guest lectures and hands-on workshops with industry specialists.

10+ Years of Excellence

Since inception, VIT TechFest has inspired over 50,000 students across the country, providing them with technical resources, mentorship, and placement prospects in top engineering enterprises.
